Saropa Workspace is a free productivity tool, released under the MIT license.
It will always be free to use.

If your team needs more than the extension itself provides, Saropa offers
paid engineering services around developer tooling and workflow automation.

## What We Build

### Custom VS Code Extension Development

Bespoke extensions for your team's editor: tree views, custom commands,
webview panels, language tooling, and integrations with your internal
services. If you have a repetitive editor workflow, we can turn it into a
first-class extension.

### Developer-Workflow Automation

Scripts, task runners, and glue code that remove manual steps from your
day-to-day: build and release pipelines, code generation, repository
maintenance, and onboarding setup. We design these to be readable and
maintainable by your own team after handoff.

### Bespoke Tooling

Standalone command-line tools, internal utilities, and one-off migrations.
We scope the work tightly, deliver it with documentation, and hand it back
in a form your team owns outright.

## How We Work

- **Scoped engagements.** We agree on a concrete deliverable up front and
  ship it.
- **You own the result.** Code we write for you is yours, with documentation,
  so your team can maintain it without us.
- **Practical, not flashy.** We build the simplest thing that solves the
  problem, matched to your existing conventions.
